The McNaughton Centre

The McNaughton Learning Resource Centre is IEEE UOIT’s club space, located on the 5th floor of ACE.

The Centre has been in development since 2013, and finally, with help of our donors, UOIT, and dedicated team members, it will finally be offered as a fully functional lab space.

The McNaughton Centre’s primary function is to provide students with access to resources such as tools, components, reading material, and peer support.

Paid IEEE members will receive full access to the Centre.
