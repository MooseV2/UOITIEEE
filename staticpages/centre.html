The McNaughton Centre

The McNaughton Learning Resource Centre is IEEE UOITâ€™s lab space, located on the second floor of SIRC.

The Centre has been in development since 2013, its function is to provide students with resources such as tools, components, reading material, and peer support for DIY projects, and learning.

Hands-on practical workshops for student members are hosted regularly in the space, where students learn how to use Arduinos, Raspberry Pis, and learn the basics of breadboard prototyping and programming.

Paid IEEE Student Members will receive full access to the Centre.
